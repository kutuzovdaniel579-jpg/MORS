About MORS

MORS · LATIN FOR DEATH · A TRIBUTE TO PURE HELLFIRE FPS

What is MORS?

MORS is a retro‑styled first‑person shooter that channels the raw, relentless energy of the original DOOM era. No hand‑holding, no filler—just fast movement, brutal combat and claustrophobic levels drenched in demonic imagery.

The name comes from the Latin word for “death”, setting the tone for a world where every corridor, altar and gateway feels like a ritual gone horribly right.

Core vibe & design

MORS is built around a few simple pillars:

  • Old‑school pacing – fast strafing, circle‑strafing, no aim‑down‑sights nonsense.
  • Heavy atmosphere – Latin inscriptions, infernal symbols, and occult architecture.
  • Brutal feedback – loud weapons, chunky impacts, and enemies that feel dangerous.
  • Readable visuals – retro‑inspired, but clean enough for modern screens.

World & lore

The game takes place on the bleeding edge between a corrupted human facility and an opened infernal plane. Latin phrases, sigils and relics are woven into the environment—not as decoration, but as hints to what went wrong and what still lurks beneath.

You are not a chosen hero. You are simply the last thing standing between Mors and everything else.
